Dr Ashok Pandey
Management Board Member, GEMS Education, India Region

Dr Ashok Pandey is a globally renowned educationist with three decades of experience in school education in India and abroad as a teacher, principal, author, and motivational speaker. He is a recipient of the CBSE Award for Contribution in Education, the National Award of the President of India, the Lifetime Achievement Award of the Ferguson Humanitarian Foundation International, and many more. He is also listed among the Top 100 People Managers by Forbes India. He serves as a Member of the National Council for Teacher Education (Govt. of India); a Member of the Governing Board of the CBSE education board; a Member of the Advisory Board of Cambridge Assessment International Education (South Asia); Chairman of the National Progressive Schools Conference, an association of 200 schools countrywide and as Hon. President of the Private School Principals Association (PSPA) to promote the exchange of best pedagogical practices. His efforts in education were published as a case study by the Indian Institute of Management Ahmedabad.
